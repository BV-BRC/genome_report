Phylogenetic Analysis

The closest reference and representative genomes to {{gto.genome_name}} were identified by Mash/MinHashOndov, et al. 2016. PATRIC global protein families (PGFams)Davis, et al. 2016 were selected from these genomes to determine the phylogenetic placement of this genome. The protein sequences from these families were aligned with MUSCLEEdgar 2004, and the nucleotides for each of those sequences were mapped to the protein alignment. The joint set of amino acid and nucleotide alignments were concatenated into a data matrix, and RaxMLStamatakis 2014 was used to analyze this matrix, with fast bootstrappingStamatakis, et al. 2008 was used to generate the support values in the tree ().
